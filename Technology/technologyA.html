launch vehicle

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled vehicle used to carry payloads from Earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our WEB-X carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation. Standing 150 meters tall, it's quite an awe-inspiring sight on the launch pad!
