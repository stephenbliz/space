space capsule

A space capsule is an often screwed spacecraft that uses a blunt-body reentry capsule to enter the Earth's atmosphere without wings. Our capsule is where you'll spend your time during the flight. It includes a space gym, cinema, and plenty of other activities to keep you entertained.
